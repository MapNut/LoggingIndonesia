Deforestation

ENG

This site is designed to provide guidance on mapping logging related features on OpenStreetMap in order to capture and record where deforestation is happening and the extent. This initiative was driven by the devastating fires that took place in Kalimantan in 2015, and the desire for students of the Green School in Bali to assist their neighbours in their struggle. A Tasking Manager project was created to coordinate the mapping efforts and capture all the data in OpenStreetMap so that the data is freely available for everyone to access, edit, redistribute and use in any way they see fit.
